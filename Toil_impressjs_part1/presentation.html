Manual Work

Repetitive Work

Work that is done over and over

Reactive Work

Spend most of your time firefighting

Technical debt just piles up

Lacks Enduring Value

doing this type of work over and over provides no actual value

Often there is no sense of satisfaction from doing this work

This can lead to unexpected problems down the line... you don't have a sense of accomplishment

Can lead to problems getting missed because of Alert Fatigue

Grows As Fast As Source

toil tends to grow as fast as the source

Human resources scale almost linearly with machine resources

in the old way, add hardware & and people they scale together

Just How Bad Is TOIL???

Many think toil is terrible and you should aim for no toil... that's not the case
